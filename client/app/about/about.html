Summary

I was born and raised in Hopewell New Jersey. After high school, I attended the University of New Hampshire (UNH) with a focus in Mechanical Engineering. My first job after UNH took me to Boston where I worked for a medical device company called Waters. A few years later, I took a design job at GOSS International in New Hampshire. The recession claimed many jobs in 2009, mine included. It was at this point I co-founded a small company named JAS Energy in Blacksburg Virginia. I currently am working as an engineer at Portsmouth Naval Shipyard.

My academic interests lie in technology, business, finance, and investing. I am avidly seeking new investment opportunities within the stock market. My philosophy is that of a classic value investor following the teachings of Ben Graham, and Warren Buffet, and am always looking to buy stock of well performing stock at depressed prices. I am also interested in the world of business and business development. My most current interests lie in web development, most notably JavaScript and front end/back end frameworks. In the near future, I will be developing fully interactive, rich, and useful web applications from scratch. Please check back here often for updates.

Outside of work

When I am not busy with work or a project, I like to keep an active social life. I work out on a daily basis and believe in healthy living. I’ll play most sports, but I enjoy Ice hockey (or street) the most. Many say I have a pretty good shot, but it isn’t quite an NHL release just yet! When the weekend comes around, I like to make time after a productive day to enjoy a night out with friends. Travelling is another passion of mine. I have been overseas a few times, most notably in 2014 when I backpacked on my own through three countries. It was a great experience and I met a lot of great people along the way.
